We are a restaurant in Leominster, Massachusetts opened in 2021. It has always been the dream of our founder, Jacob DeCarolis, to open his own Italian restaurant. Being a proud Italian-American (though he knows almost nothing of actual Italian culture), Jacob is glad to be able to share his heritage with his community through cibo, and he hopes you and your family will have a great experience with us! Ciao!
