Introduction

What is the website about?

My website will be a website for a new, made-up Italian restaurant. It will include a homepage, an about page, a menu page, and a page to leave a review. The styling will roughly be modeled after the website for Il Camino in Leominster, which can be found here

What is its purpose?

The purpose of my website will be to give vital information about the restaurant and give customers an opportunity to share their thoughts and experience.

Who is the target audience?

The target audience of my website will be restaurant-goers who want to know more about the restaurant before coming.

What does the target audience want from the website?

The target audience would want ease of access and a clear layout of the website. Nothing should be too difficult for the user to understand, and all information, such as prices, should be clear.

Competitive Research

Are there other websites on your topic?

There are plenty of other websites on this topic, because every Italian restaurant likely has its own website!

What are other sites doing? What are they successful at? What are they unsuccessful at?

Looking at the websites for five different restaurants, they all have sections for the menu, and most of them offered catering and an hours/location section on their website. It didn't really seem like their was any website that did anything particularly different than any other website.

How can your site be better or different than the competitors?

A feature I could put on my website which would set it apart from the rest is having a "Meet the Staff" page. No site that I saw had this, and I feel like it's important, especially in the times we're living in, to recognize all the people that make things run smoothly behind the scenes!

All the websites that I looked at were:

  • Il Camino
  • Il Forno
  • Bertucci's
  • Linguine's
  • Antonio's
